# Content Style Guide

## Brand Voice

- **Direct and practical** – No fluff. Get to the point.
- **Compliance-first** – We diagnose and repair; we never delete or defeat emissions systems.
- **Independent** – We are not affiliated with Mercedes-Benz or any manufacturer.
- **Professional** – Dealer-level thinking, mobile delivery. No guesswork.

## Allowed Claims

- Mercedes-Benz Truck & Van trained technician (UK)
- Dealer-level diagnostic access (Xentry where applicable)
- Compliance-first approach
- Mobile diagnostics across Kent & SE London
- Written outcomes for every job
- Specialist in Mercedes Sprinter (W906, W907), Vito (W447)
- OM651, OM654 engine experience

## Disallowed Claims

- Affiliation with or endorsement by Mercedes-Benz
- "Official" or "authorised" Mercedes service
- Guarantees we cannot substantiate (e.g. "fastest", "best", "cheapest")
- Emissions deletes, DPF removal, AdBlue bypasses, or any illegal modification
- Unsubstantiated performance or outcome guarantees

## Compliance-First Wording Snippets

Use these phrases consistently on emissions-related content:

- "We diagnose and repair - we never delete, bypass, or defeat emissions systems."
- "No DPF removal, no AdBlue bypasses, no EGR blanking, no defeat devices."
- "DPF removal is almost always illegal for road vehicles in the UK."
- "Compliance-first: we fix it properly, not bypass it."
- "We help you stay MOT-compliant, ULEZ-compliant, and on the right side of the law."

## Service Page Template

1. **Hero** – Service name, compliance badge if emissions-related
2. **Intro** – One paragraph, primary keyword in first 100 words
3. **Is this the right service?** – Qualification box
4. **Typical causes / symptoms** – Bullet list
5. **Compliance notice** – On every emissions-adjacent page
6. **What we do** – Bullet list
7. **What we don't do** – Where applicable
8. **Pricing** – Zone table, link to /pricing
9. **FAQs** – 4–8 page-specific questions
10. **Related articles** – Blog posts
11. **Related services** – Cross-sell
12. **Footer CTA** – Book, WhatsApp, Phone

## Blog Article Template

1. **Title** – Include primary keyword
2. **Meta description** – 150–160 chars, keyword + CTA
3. **Intro** – Hook, problem statement
4. **Sections** – H2s with clear structure
5. **"Need help with this?"** – CTA linking to relevant service page
6. **Related services** – Sidebar links
